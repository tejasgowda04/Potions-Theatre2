[ Our Story ]

Born from a Shared Love
of Storytelling

Potions Cocktail Theatre was born from a vision — to create a space where every drink has a narrative, every plate tells a story, and every evening becomes an unforgettable memory.

Founded by Sunny Leone and Sahil Baweja at the iconic Ambawatta One, Delhi — with the historic Qutub Minar as our timeless backdrop — Potions is not just a bar. It's a stage where culinary arts and mixology perform a nightly symphony.

"Every evening is a performance.
You are our honoured guest."

Sunny Leone · Sahil Baweja
